Pesto pasta

This is pesto is made from pine nuts, parmeggiano reggiano, gran padano, olive oil, basil and garlic blended up together to create a thick sauce. I then saute with some onions, garlic, (maybe some chilli), and some red wine cooked off on a high heat. I add in some vegetables (peppers, brocolli, tomatoes, anything really) and cook these until very tender. Then add spaghetti, pesto and small bit of pasta water and mix the pasta and sauce until water is cooked off and sauce is mixed fully.

Ingredients:

  • 80g basil
  • 30g Parmeggiano Reggiano + 30g Gran Padano
  • 100g pine nuts
  • 150ml Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Spaghetti
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Splash of red wine
  • Few garlic cloves
  • Chillies

Instructions:

  1. Toast pine nuts in pan, very low heat, constant tossing, few mins until brown and fragrant.
  2. Blend in blender with basil, olive oil, parmeggiano reggiano, gran padano, and garlic until a thick paste. Salt to taste.
  3. Brown onions, garlic and chillies in olive oil. Add red wine, cook off on high heat. Add cherry tomatoes. Cook in excess EVOO until tender.
  4. Meanwhile boil spaghetti in very salty water.
  6. Add spaghetti to pan with tomatoes, with some pasta water, cook off then add some pesto and mix until fully coated.
  7. Serve hot, sprinkle of cheese and basil leaves to garnish.
